History & Brand Story

Minto was founded in 2011 as a character studio with the mission to bring positivity to digital conversations through chat stickers. Today, we operate as a creative agency network in 4 offices across 4 countries. In the course of this growth, we changed the way the world communicate forever with our character stickers, and now the way brands communicate to their clients with our Character Marketing solution.
Despite massive changes that have occurred over the years, we honor our original mission by practicing a work culture that deeply respect and care about our people and clients. We operate with that same commitment everyday, with every client.
