Kin to bn sc thng hiu bng ngn ng sng ng ca Character Marketing
05/24/2023
by Admin

Cng s thay i th hiu v s ra i ca cc xu hng marketing mi, dng nh cc linh vt thng hiu dn b lng qun bi cc marketer. Liu thi "vng son" ca cc linh vt qua hay do marketer cha bit cch bin ha chng theo nhng din mo mi? tm p n cho cu hi ny, anh Shinto, Gim c Khu vc ng Nam ca Minto c bui Talkshow cng Advertising Vietnam.
Trong s cc agency thuc lnh vc Character Marketing, Minto c bit n vi nhng thit k sticker "quen mt" vi ngi dng trn cc nn tng nhn tin ti Nht Bn, Trung Quc, Thi Lan v Vit Nam, nh Mimi and Neko, Piyomaru, Usagyuuun,... Bn cnh , Minto cn l cng ty s hu lng download stickers nhiu nht th gii (hn 5 t lt download), cho ra i gn 2.000 mascot v sticker vi trn 10 t lt tng tc mi nm.

Khi th trng ngy cng pht trin, thng ip ngi tiu dng tip nhn hng ngy gn nh khng gii hn. H tr nn bo ha vi cc cch thc marketing truyn thng. Trong bi cnh th trng nhn nhp nh hin nay, Character Marketing c th l mt hng i khc bit cc nhn hng to bc t ph. Hy cng lng nghe nhng chia s t anh Shinto khm ph nhng tim nng cng nh cch thc ng dng Character Marketing vo cc chin lc ca thng hiu.

Trong mt th gii m mi ngi tng tc vi nhau qua nhn vt o nh metaverse th vic ng dng character marketing l tt yu. Ti tin rng c rt nhiu cch nhn hng c th ng dng character marketing vo metaverse. Mt v d n gin, ngi dng s i do trong metaverse vi cc avatar. Ti Nht, chng ti k vng mi ti khon ngi dng s s hu 10 avatar. Hy th tng tng bn to nn nhng avatar vi linh vt thng hiu ca mnh, v khi hng triu ngi s dng n i do trong metaverse, bn thn vic l marketing ri.
