Definition

Character Marketing is a strategic process that leverages market research, target audience psychology, and brand attributes to craft a compelling brand mascot. This mascot is seamlessly integrated into both online and offline post-launch activities, serving as the emblematic embodiment of the brand's identity. The mascot's development involves the artful creation of a character's universe, often achieved through dynamic platforms such as social media marketing, digital content production, character branding, stickers, offline events, and a range of merchandise featuring the character's image.
